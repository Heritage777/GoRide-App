Privacy Policy

At GoRïde, we collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and precise GPS location to facilitate safe and efficient rides. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data.

  • Location Data: Collected even when the app is in the background during a trip for safety.
  • Data Sharing: Only shared with the assigned driver or emergency services.
  • Retention: Data is kept for the duration of your account activity plus legal statutory periods.

Cookie Policy

We use cookies to enhance your experience. These are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences, like your favorite pickup points in Ibadan or Lagos.

By using our platform, you consent to essential cookies used for security and functional cookies used for performance analytics.
